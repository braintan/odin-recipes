A Simple Hummus Recipe

This is a recipe for hummus

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Tahini

Steps

  1. Blend chickpeas in food processor
  2. Add oil, lemon, tahini, and salt to taste
  3. Adjust consistency with water if necessary
  4. Garnish with parsley (optional)
